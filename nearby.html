Places to visit near Jim Corbett National Park

Enjoyed safari in the park? Want to extend your stay?
Here are some other places to visit near Jim Corbett national park that you can imagine of near the park that you can choose to visit.

Corbett Museum : Bungalow of hunter Jim Corbett

1. Nainital

Nainital, the charming Himalayan lake town, is a picture-postcard perfect hill-station and one of the most popular in Northern India. One of the most best hill stations in India, Nainital allures with its pear-shaped lake, where you can go anytime throughout the day, sunsets however are the most beautiful. The town is centred around the emerald mountain lake Naini, which on most days is dotted with colourful sailboats.With all important amenities, abundance of nature’s beauty, and plenty of things to do in Nainital, it is also a good place to plan a small vacation with kids. Among other places to visit near Jim Corbett Park that are close to Nainital include the Tiffin Top Point and the Snow View Point.

Distance from Jim Corbett National Park: 65 km.

Popular Things to do in Nainital:

  • Boat ride on Naini Lake, which is among the famous places to visit in Nainital
  • Vantage points like Snow View Point and Tiffin Top
  • Ropeway ride on your trip to Nainital






















2. Bhimtal

Bhimtal is a pretty little hill town in the Lake District of Kumaon in the foothills of the Himalayas. The lake, which has a little island in the middle, is the largest of the lakes here. The climate is wonderful, spring and autumn are beautiful, summer is pleasant, and winter is sunny and bracing. Even the monsoon is lovely – misty and green. Just 20 km away from Nainital, Bhimtal too is known for its lake and the dam built on it. A small islet on the lake has an aquarium, which is a popular tourist attraction here.

Distance from Jim Corbett National Park: 79.2 km

Popular Things to do in Bhimtal:

  • Boat ride and fishing on Bhimtal Lake
  • Paragliding, rock climbing, and rappelling
  • Visit Aquarium Island Cafe by The Lake Hill
  • Visit Folk Cultural Museum and Art Museums



    •



















3. Bhowali

Located in Kumaon, Bhowali lies in the exotic regions at a height of 1704 metres. Situated right in the middle of the town of Nainital, Bhowali is known to be the orchard country, as there are various fruits here, namely peaches, apples, strawberries, apricots, and plums in huge numbers.The place during winters is phenomenal, impressing tourists with its snow-white scenery.Bhowali is a beautiful hill station that cradles in the lap of another popular and scenically blessed hill station - Nainital, which lies just 11 km away.

Distance from Jim Corbett National Park: 80 km

Popular places to visit in Bhowali:

  • Golu Devta Temple
  • Shyamkhet Tea Garden
  • Kainchi Dham



    •


















4. Sitabani Forest

Sitabani Forest Reserve is opposite to the famous Jim Corbett National Park. This naturally enclosed place is nestled in the foothills of the mighty Himalayas. The dense Sal forests of Sitabani Forest Reserve is a home to varied wildlife like tigers, leopards, elephants, spotted deer, sambhar, wild boar etc.Sitabani forest zone is very popular for its rich diversity of flora. There are more than 600 species of trees, shrubs, bamboos, herbs, grasses, orchids, woody climbers, and wetland vegetation.

Distance from Jim Corbett National Park: 26.2 km

Popular things to do in Sitabani Forest Reserve :

  • Bird watching
  • Witnessing various species of bamboos
  • Jeep Safari at this Wildlife Reserve from Sitabani Forest Department
  • Visit the historical Forest Rest House of Sitabani



    •


















5. Corbett Museum

The Corbett museum is one of the most captivating parts of the dense Corbett Reserve beautifully located in Kaladhungi and is positioned just 3 km away from its main town. The museum is actually a heritage bungalow of Jim Corbett, the renowned hunter turned environmentalist who raised the value of wildlife conservation. The best time to visit this museum is during the months of January and March.
The timings for visiting this museum is between 7.00 A.M. to 6 P.M.

Distance from Jim Corbett National Park: 29 km

Popular things in Corbett Museum :

  • Purchase of Handmade Rural items
  • Reflect the images of tiger and other wild animals.
  • Personal articles, paintings, sketches
