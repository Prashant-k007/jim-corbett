EDUCATIONAL TOUR

An educational tour offers students of all Classes an opportunity for a rich immersion experience. While an educational tour is great fun, it differs from a vacation. Typically led by a teacher and group chaperones , such trips provide invaluable global learning.

Objectives of Educational Tour :-                     Advantages of Educational Tour :-

  1. Effective Learning                           1. Improves critical thinking skills

  2. Personal Development                        2 .Student's worldview is expanded

  3. Respect for Culture                          3. Develops confidence

  4. Deepen Social and Historical Knowledge                  4. Provides a wider perspective

The Educational Study Tour packages to Jim Corbett include stay on triple/ Quad sharing stay options, food, jeep/ elephant/ camel safari, adventure activities, team building, trekking and many more fun filled yet educative referenced activities. Temperature drops in foothills of Himalaya in the eveing, is perfect time to sit and absorb the wowness of weather. Many interactive sessions sitting around a warm bonfire and playing a little music tends to act as an icebreaker between people which can help in creating many social networks amongst students.
